Frequently Asked Questions about Bradicoin Loan

Do I need to have Bradicoin to apply for a loan?
No! That's exactly our biggest differentiator. You can use other assets as collateral and receive the loan value in Bradicoin.
What is KYC and why do I need to go through this process?
KYC stands for Know Your Customer. It's a mandatory identity verification process to ensure security, prevent fraud, and comply with legal regulations.
How does the Score system work?
The Bradicoin Loan Score rewards consistent platform use. More loans paid correctly = higher Score = lower rates and better conditions.
What's the difference between Turbo Loan and Investor-to-Investor Loan?
Turbo Loan uses platform's own resources with instant approval. Investor-to-Investor connects you with investors who offer personalized conditions.
What types of collateral do you accept?
We accept different types of assets, from physical assets with recognized market value to other digital assets. Contact us for details.
How long does it take to receive the Bradicoins after approval?
After confirmation of your collateral and loan approval, the Bradicoins are released to your digital wallet in just a few minutes.
